# Personal Investment Mandate

## 1. Objective & Philosophy
The goal of this vault is to build a compoundable, research-driven framework for long-term wealth accumulation. The philosophy relies on rigorous fundamental analysis, disciplined position sizing, and a multi-year ownership mindset.

## 2. Capital Allocation & Funding Schedule
*   **Initial Seed Capital:** $500 AUD
*   **Systematic Contribution:** $200 AUD per month (Baseline)
*   **Flexible Funding Range:** Minimum $100 AUD / Maximum $500 AUD per month, adjusted based on market opportunities or capital availability.
*   **Hard Cap:** No capital outside this defined schedule may be injected without a formal mandate revision.

## 3. Investment Universe
*   **Primary Focus:** Long-only equities listed on United States (US) and Australian (AU) exchanges.
*   **Asset Allocation:** Permissible mix of broad-market Index Funds / ETFs (Core) and individual equities (Satellites).
*   **Excluded Assets:** No active trading of Forex, Commodities, Cryptocurrencies, or Derivatives (Options/Futures). Macro trends in these spaces are limited to passive reading.

## 4. Position Sizing & Portfolio Architecture
*   **Maximum Single Equity Limit:** No individual stock position may exceed **12%** of the total portfolio value at the time of cost-allocation.
*   **Core Allocation:** Broad market ETFs may exceed this limit to act as the portfolio baseline.

## 5. Risk Mitigation & Triggers
*   **Flagged Status (-10% Drawdown):** Any single equity position down 10% from cost basis is flagged for heavy monitoring. This triggers an immediate review to determine if the market is mispricing the asset (potential to double-down) or if structural risks are emerging.
*   **Hard Re-evaluation Trigger (-15% Drawdown):** Any single equity position down 15% or more from cost basis mandates a formal written re-assessment memo. The position must either be resized down, sold completely, or explicitly defended based on unchanged fundamental value.

## 6. Time Horizon & Benchmarking
*   **Minimum Evaluation Period:** 12 Months. No position will be structurally judged or exited based on short-term price volatility before this mark, unless the core thesis breaks.
*   **Target Horizon:** 2 to 3+ years for structural compounders.
*   **Primary Benchmarks:** 
    *   S&P 500 Index (SPY) for US exposures.
    *   ASX 200 Total Return Index for AU exposures.

## 7. Tax & Execution Disciplines
*   **Australian CGT Efficiency:** To optimize for Australian tax law, positions should ideally be held for a minimum of 366 days to qualify for the **50% Capital Gains Tax (CGT) discount**. 
*   **Tax Logging:** Every realization event (sale) must log estimated tax liabilities to avoid end-of-financial-year surprises.
