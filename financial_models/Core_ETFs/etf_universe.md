# Core ETF Universe: Foundational Asset Allocation

This document tracks the broad-market index funds utilized as the "Core" wealth foundation of the portfolio, absorbing regular systematic monthly capital injections outside of high-conviction satellite allocations.

## 🇦🇺 Australian Core Equities (ASX Tracking)

### 1. Vanguard Australian Shares Index ETF (ASX: VAS)
*   **What it tracks:** The ASX 300 (The top 300 largest companies in Australia).
*   **Management Fee (MER):** 0.07% p.a.
*   **Core Characteristics:** Heavily weighted toward top tier-1 Australian Banks (CBA, NAB) and Resources (BHP). Provides high, fully franked dividend income.
*   **Mandate Fit:** Acts as our direct local benchmark baseline.

### 2. Betashares Australia 200 ETF (ASX: A200)
*   **What it tracks:** The ASX 200 (The top 200 companies).
*   **Management Fee (MER):** 0.04% p.a. (The cheapest structural option in Australia).
*   **Core Characteristics:** Slightly more concentrated than VAS, but provides nearly identical financial returns with lower fees.

## 🇺🇸 & Global Core Equities (International Growth Tracking)

### 3. Vanguard U.S. Total Market Shares Index ETF (ASX: VTS)
*   **What it tracks:** The entire US stock market (CRSP US Total Market Index).
*   **Management Fee (MER):** 0.03% p.a.
*   **Core Characteristics:** Maximizes exposure to US mega-cap tech giants (Microsoft, Apple, Nvidia, Amazon). Note: Requires a W-8BEN form for tax management.

### 4. Vanguard MSCI International Shares Index ETF (ASX: VGS)
*   **What it tracks:** The MSCI World ex-Australia Index (Over 1,500 developed world global giants).
*   **Management Fee (MER):** 0.18% p.a.
*   **Core Characteristics:** Fully domiciled in Australia (no extra tax paperwork).

---

## 🧭 Asset Allocation Strategy (Core/Satellite Target)

*   **The Baseline Engine:** 70% to 80% of total portfolio capital flows systematically into a combination of **[VAS or A200]** and **[VGS or VTS]** to capture market-average returns.
*   **The Alpha Satellite Engine:** 20% to 30% maximum combined capital is unlocked for high-conviction individual stock memos (AAPL, BHP), capped strictly at **12% per position** at the time of purchase.
